Bloodborne Lore

The main goal of the "Bloodborne Lore" project is to look through the following vast amounts of data that are in the game:

  • Item descriptions
  • Npc dialogue
  • Boss descriptions
  • Weapon descriptions

There are of course more data points than what was just listed but that is the jist.

We hope to find correlations between all the data we have gathered in order to find which data points hold the most significance in relation to the other data points.
